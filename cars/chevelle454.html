Chevelle SS 454

Chevrolet · 1970

The high-water mark of muscle.

The LS6 Chevelle is widely regarded as the most powerful production muscle car of the classic era — 450 horses, factory-rated and understated.

Cowl-induction hood, twin stripes, and a torque figure that bent driveshafts.

This profile preserves her not as merchandise, but as memory.
