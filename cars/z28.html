Camaro Z/28

Born to go racing on Sunday.

The Z/28 existed for one reason: to win Trans-Am. A high-revving 302, four-wheel discs, and a chassis sharpened for corners.

The thinking enthusiast’s muscle car — balance over brute force.

This profile preserves her not as merchandise, but as memory.
