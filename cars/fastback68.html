Ford · 1968

The one Bullitt made immortal.

A black 390 GT in 1968 San Francisco. Ten minutes of cinema that changed muscle car history forever. The Fastback roofline, the V8 growl, the silence between corners. Steve McQueen simply drove — the car did the rest.

This profile preserves her not as merchandise, but as memory.
