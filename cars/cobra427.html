Shelby · 1965

A wristwatch with a sledgehammer inside.

A featherweight British roadster wrapped around the biggest American V8 Shelby could bolt in. Brutal, raw, and barely tamed.

Few cars have ever offered so much power with so little between you and it.

This profile preserves her not as merchandise, but as memory.
