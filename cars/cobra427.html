Cobra 427

Shelby · 1965

Four hundred and twenty-five horsepower. No roof.

A 427 cubic inch side-oiler V8 in a car that weighed 2,350 pounds. Carroll Shelby asked AC to make the bodywork bigger. The physics did the rest. Zero to sixty in under four seconds in 1965.

This profile preserves her not as merchandise, but as memory.
