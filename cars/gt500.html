Shelby GT500 “Eleanor”

Ford · 1967

The grey ghost of the boulevard.

Wide-mouthed and low, finished in primer-grey menace, she is the silhouette an entire generation recognises before the badge ever comes into view. Carroll Shelby's racing obsession married to brute American displacement.

This profile preserves her not as merchandise, but as memory.
